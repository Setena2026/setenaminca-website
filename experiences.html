EXPERIENCES

Experience the Sierra Nevada

Adventure, nature and authentic moments designed to connect you with one of Colombia's most extraordinary destinations.

More Than a Stay

Every experience has been thoughtfully selected to help you discover the culture, biodiversity and landscapes surrounding Se'Tena Tierra Húmeda.

ADVENTURE

Cerro Kennedy

Discover one of the most spectacular viewpoints in the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta. This unforgettable journey combines breathtaking landscapes, mountain forests and extraordinary birdlife.

On clear mornings, enjoy panoramic views where the snow-capped peaks meet the Caribbean Sea—a truly unique natural spectacle.

NATURE

Birdwatching

The Sierra Nevada is recognized worldwide as one of the richest birdwatching destinations, home to hundreds of endemic and migratory species.

Whether you are an experienced birder or simply enjoy nature, every walk offers remarkable opportunities for observation and photography.

GASTRONOMY

Farm-to-Table Dining

Enjoy a dining experience inspired by fresh local ingredients, seasonal flavors and the traditions of the Sierra Nevada.

Every meal celebrates sustainability, simplicity and the authentic taste of the region.

WELLNESS

Relax & Reconnect

Slow down and embrace the peaceful rhythm of the rainforest. Read in a hammock, listen to the river, enjoy a wellness treatment or simply reconnect with nature.

Sometimes the greatest experience is allowing yourself to pause.

Create Your Own Experience

Every stay at Se'Tena is an opportunity to explore, reconnect and create unforgettable memories in the Sierra Nevada.
