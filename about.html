About Us

Rooted in nature. Designed for comfort. Created for connection.

OUR STORY

A place with history, purpose and meaning.

Se'Tena means "Wet Land" in the Damana language, one of the ancestral languages of the Indigenous communities of the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta.

The land was named by Mamo José Gil, who recognized this place for its spiritual significance. For generations, this territory has been a space of respect, connection and harmony with nature.

Today, Se'Tena continues that legacy by offering an experience where comfort and nature coexist, inviting guests to slow down, reconnect and create meaningful moments.
