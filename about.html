About Us

More than a destination, Se'Tena is a place where comfort, nature and meaningful human connections become one experience.

Our Story

A place with history, purpose and meaning.

Se'Tena means "Wet Land" in the Damana language, one of the ancestral languages of the Indigenous communities of the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta.

The land was named by Mamo José Gil, who recognized this place for its spiritual significance. Today, that heritage inspires a sanctuary where comfort, nature and meaningful human connections exist in harmony.
